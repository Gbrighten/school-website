Our Facilities

Modern infrastructure and supportive campus facilities for a safe, healthy and enriching learning experience.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive teaching with digital boards, projectors, and modern learning aids.

Library

A well-stocked library with textbooks, storybooks, reference materials and magazines.

Computer education with latest systems and practical training for digital literacy.

Separate laboratories for Physics, Chemistry and Biology with safe equipment and experiments.

Clean filtered drinking water available throughout the campus.

CCTV surveillance, a secure campus and trained staff ensuring student safety.
