Admissions

Admissions are now open for the academic session 2026-27. We welcome students from Pre KG to X Std only, serving local families with affordable fees in our caring village school.

Admission Procedure

  1. Collect the application form from the school office or download it from the website.
  2. Complete the form and attach the required documents.
  3. Submit the form to the school office and attend the interview/assessment if necessary.
  4. Receive confirmation and complete the admission process with fee payment.

Eligibility

  • Pre-KG and KG students must meet the age criteria as per school rules.
  • Admission to higher classes depends on previous academic records and school availability.
  • All students are expected to maintain good behaviour and learning attitude.

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school transfer certificate
  • Report cards or mark sheets
  • Passport size photographs
  • Proof of address

Office Timings

  • Monday – Friday: 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed
